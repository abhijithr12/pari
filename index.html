What is PARICHAY?

Parichay is a TECH FEST organized by the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) department of T John Institute of Technology. The event aims to ignite student's passion for technology by providing a platform to explore, innovate, and compete in various technical domains. Through engaging competitions, workshops, and interactive sessions, Parichay fosters a culture of learning, creativity, and problem-solving, encouraging students to enhance their technical knowledge and skills.
