CodeStorm 2025
Hackathon Round 1: Abstract Submission
The first round of our intercollegiate hackathon will kick off on March 9, 2025, where participating teams will receive a problem statement. Teams must analyze the challenge and submit an abstract outlining their proposed solution by March 11, 2025.

Abstract Requirements
The abstract should include:
  • Team Details: Team name and member details
  • Problem Understanding: A brief explanation of the problem statement
  • Proposed Solution: An overview of the approach and key features
  • Technology Stack: Tools, frameworks, or programming languages planned for use
  • Innovation & Feasibility: How the solution stands out and its practicality
  • Expected Outcome: The impact and effectiveness of the solution
After evaluation, shortlisted teams will move to Round 2, where they will have 48 hours to develop a working prototype of their solution.
CodeStorm 2025
Hackathon Round 2: Prototype Development
The shortlisted teams from Round 1 will be announced within two days of the abstract submission deadline. These teams will proceed to Round 2, where they will have 48 hours to develop a functional prototype based on their proposed solution.

Prototype Submission Details
  • Teams must code and submit their prototype within the given time.
  • Submission will be online via a Git repository link.
  • The prototype should include a README file with setup instructions and usage details.
The best solutions will be judged on functionality, innovation, scalability, and impact. Good luck to all teams!
