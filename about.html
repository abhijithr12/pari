AI MINDS

AI Minds is a dynamic and innovative club under the Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) department of T John Institute of Technology. It serves as a hub for students passionate about AI, machine learning, and data science, providing a collaborative space to learn, research, and develop cutting-edge technologies. Through workshops, hackathons, and expert sessions, AI Minds encourages members to explore real-world applications of AI, enhance their problem-solving skills, and stay ahead in the ever-evolving field of artificial intelligence. The club fosters a culture of creativity, teamwork, and technical excellence, preparing students for careers in AI-driven industries.

OUR HOD

Dr. Asha S. Manek, B.E, M.E, Ph.D., is a Professor & HOD in the Department of AI & ML at T John Institute of Technology, Bengaluru, with 28 years of teaching experience. She holds a BE from Nagpur University, an ME from Bangalore University, and a PhD from JNT University, Hyderabad. She has published 45+ research papers in reputed journals, including Scopus, SCI, IEEE Access, and Springer, and has received multiple awards, including the Best Paper Award at ICECMSN-2021. Her research interests include Data Mining, Machine Learning, Web Mining, IoT, Cloud Computing, and Wireless Sensor Networks. She has guided 45+ UG & PG projects, authored a book on C programming, published four patents, two copyrights, and organized numerous conferences and workshops. She has served as a reviewer, judge, resource person, and advisory committee member in national and international events and received recognition as a Primary Evaluator for Toycathon 2021 by MIC AICTE, India. She is a lifetime member of ISTE and has previously been a member of IEEE and CSI.

ORGANIZER

Additionally, she has completed a Software Testing course at Cegonsoft Technologies.
